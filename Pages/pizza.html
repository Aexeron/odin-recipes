Pizza

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Toppings of your choice
  • Mozzarella cheese

Method

  1. Prepare Your Surface With Oil
  2. Shape the dough
  3. Add toppings and cheese
  4. Put it in the pizza pan
  5. Put it in the oven at 230 degrees C for 20 minutes
